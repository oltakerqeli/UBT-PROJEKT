About Zayn Malik

Zayn Malik, a global icon and former member of One Direction, has captivated audiences worldwide with his soulful voice and dynamic artistry. Born on January 12, 1993, in Bradford, England, Zayn rose to fame as part of the legendary boy band before pursuing a solo career in 2015. His debut solo album, Mind of Mine, topped charts globally, featuring hits like "Pillowtalk."

Stairway to the Sky Tour 2024

Zayn is currently embarking on his first-ever solo tour, the Stairway to the Sky Tour, celebrating his latest album, Room Under the Stairs. The tour spans iconic venues across the UK and the US, including the O2 Academy in Leeds, Hammerstein Ballroom in New York, and the Shrine Auditorium in Los Angeles. This tour is a celebration of Zayn’s evolution as an artist, showcasing fan-favorite hits, acoustic renditions, and new music from his latest release.

Known for creating a deeply personal connection with his audience, Zayn promises surprises, unique stage setups, and even guest appearances throughout the tour. Fans are raving about the exclusive "Stairway to the Sky" merchandise, which includes hoodies and posters.
